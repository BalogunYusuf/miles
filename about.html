About JW Courtship Advisory

Founded by members of the congregation who understood the unique challenges of finding spiritually compatible partners in today's world.

Our Mission

Serving the Brotherhood
with Integrity

JW Courtship Advisory was founded in 2018 by a small team of brothers and sisters who saw a genuine need within the congregation — spiritually-minded individuals seeking courtship but finding limited, dignified options that respected their values.


We believe courtship is sacred and that the process of finding a lifelong companion deserves the same intentionality and reverence we bring to our spiritual lives. Our consultants are all active members of the JW community who bring both personal experience and professional discretion to every introduction.


We reject the impersonal nature of algorithm-driven apps. Instead, we invest time in understanding each person — their character, their faith, their goals — and we only make introductions we believe in genuinely.
